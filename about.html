About Albright Concrete

A family-owned concrete company rooted in integrity, craftsmanship, and four generations of hands-on experience.

Our Founder

Donald Wayne Albright

Donald Wayne Albright was born on May 16, 1931, in Nebraska. He married Coleen McCormick in Moberly, Missouri, in 1954. Donald proudly served his country during the Korean War as a member of the United States Coast Guard.

In the 1970s, he founded Albright Concrete, establishing a business built on hard work, quality craftsmanship, honesty, and dedication to customers. His vision created a family legacy that continues today through four generations of concrete professionals.

Donald Wayne Albright passed away on March 20, 2022, but his commitment to excellence continues to guide the company and the way Albright Concrete serves Mid-Missouri.

Historical Timeline

  • 1931 — Donald Wayne Albright is born in Nebraska.
  • 1954 — Marries Coleen McCormick in Moberly, Missouri.
  • Korean War — Serves in the United States Coast Guard.
  • 1970s — Founds Albright Concrete in Moberly, Missouri.
  • 1970s–2000s — Builds a reputation for honest, high-quality concrete work.
  • 2022 — Donald passes away; his legacy lives on through his family and business.

Family Legacy

What began as one man’s commitment to doing the job right has grown into four generations of concrete experience. The Albright family remains hands-on in the business, honoring Donald’s standards of integrity, craftsmanship, and service on every job in Moberly and the surrounding Mid-Missouri area.

Donald Wayne Albright, founder of Albright Concrete, with his wife Coleen — the beginning of a four-generation concrete legacy.

Our Owner

Joshua Albright

Joshua Albright was born on May 30, 1985, and is the grandson of company founder Donald Wayne Albright. Growing up around concrete, he learned the trade directly from his family and developed a deep respect for doing things the right way.

As the current owner of Albright Concrete, Joshua continues the family tradition of providing dependable, high-quality concrete services throughout Mid-Missouri. He leads every project with a focus on craftsmanship, communication, and long-term performance.

Leadership & Values

  • Craftsmanship: Every driveway, patio, and slab is treated as if it were his own property.
  • Customer Service: Clear, honest communication from estimate to final walkthrough.
  • Integrity: Doing what’s promised, standing behind the work, and respecting your home or jobsite.
  • Professional Excellence: Safe, efficient job sites with a well-trained crew.

Continuing a Four-Generation Legacy

Under Joshua’s leadership, Albright Concrete remains a trusted concrete contractor in Moberly, Missouri. The company still carries the same values Donald instilled in the 1970s: work hard, be honest, and deliver concrete work that lasts for decades.
